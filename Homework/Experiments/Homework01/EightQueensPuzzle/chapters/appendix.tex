\section{复现实验命令}

\begin{lstlisting}[language=bash,caption={八皇后实验复现命令},label=lst:reproduce]
python experiments/eight_queens_benchmark.py --n 8 --repeat 3
\end{lstlisting}

默认输出路径为 \texttt{experiments/eight\_queens\_benchmark.json}。
